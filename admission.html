Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility

All courses are open to women and girls from our target communities. There is no minimum educational qualification required.

2. Course Fee

All our training programs are provided completely free of cost. However, a small, refundable security deposit may be required to ensure commitment.

3. Attendance

A minimum of 80% attendance is required to be eligible for the course completion certificate.

4. Materials

All necessary art supplies and materials will be provided by Mithila KritiKala Foundation.

5. Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain a respectful and collaborative environment. Any form of indiscipline may result in discontinuation from the course.

6. Certification

A certificate of completion will be awarded upon successful completion of the course, including any final projects or assessments.
